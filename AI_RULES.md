# AI Rules

- 始终中英文书写，表达直接、简洁、专业。  
  Always write in both Chinese and English, with direct, concise, professional wording.

- 先理解结构，再动代码；优先解决根因，不修表象。  
  Understand the structure before editing code; fix root causes, not surface symptoms.

- 保持技术栈一致：Next.js App Router、React、TypeScript、Tailwind、MDX、`pnpm`。  
  Keep the stack consistent: Next.js App Router, React, TypeScript, Tailwind, MDX, and `pnpm`.

- 类型必须准确，避免 `any`，避免含糊实现。  
  Keep types accurate, avoid `any`, and avoid vague implementations.

- 页面级问题放 `page.tsx`，交互和局部展示放组件。  
  Put page-level concerns in `page.tsx`; keep interaction and local presentation in components.

- SEO 不止是 metadata，重要页面必须有可见正文。  
  SEO is not only metadata; important pages must include visible on-page content.

- 移动端友好是默认要求，不是补充要求。  
  Mobile friendliness is a default requirement, not an optional follow-up.

- 全站统一的是 `font-family`；标题和正文可以有不同字号与字重。  
  What must stay consistent site-wide is `font-family`; headings and body text may use different sizes and weights.

- 不经明确批准，不提交、不发生产、不随意改全局配置。  
  Without explicit approval, do not commit, deploy to production, or casually change global configuration.

- 修改后做最小必要验证，至少保证类型、结构和影响范围可控。  
  After changes, do the minimum necessary validation and ensure types, structure, and scope remain under control.
